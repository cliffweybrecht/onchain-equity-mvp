# Process Assumptions & Human Invariants

## Assumed Invariants
The following are assumed but not enforced:
- Correct execution of attestation tooling
- Proper key custody by maintainers
- Intentional inclusion of relevant artifacts
- Accurate labeling of evidence scope

## Non-Guarantees
The system does not guarantee:
- Completeness of governance documentation
- Absence of operator error
- Correctness of human judgment
- Procedural compliance outside recorded evidence

## Incident Interpretation
Evidence should be interpreted as:
- A cryptographic record of what was asserted
- Not proof of intent, diligence, or compliance
