# Audit Boundary Clarification

## Meaning of "Audit-Ready"
Within this repository, "audit-ready" means:
- Evidence is immutable once frozen
- Verification is deterministic and offline
- Artifacts can be independently validated

## What "Audit-Ready" Does NOT Mean
- Regulatory approval
- Legal sufficiency
- Third-party certification
- Absence of governance risk

## Reviewer Responsibility
Auditors and regulators are responsible for:
- Determining relevance to their framework
- Assessing trust in issuers
- Mapping evidence to external standards
